Our Services

Here are the services that we offer and a description of each service.

If you need a self-service laundry option, we offer a wide selection of machines at our lavanderia to avoid waiting. We have large commercial machines for your heavy loads along with soap and laundry bags available for purchase.

If you want us to do your laundry just leave your clothes with us and we will wash and dry your laundry for you. This laundry service is available at our Seattle and Burien laundromats.

If you are looking for the ultimate time saver then try our pickup and delivery service. Schedule a pickup and we will come to your home to pick up your laundry. We will gently wash, dry, fold, and package your laundry using eco-friendly detergents and then return it to you, ready to wear!
